Private Google Meet Room with inspect element

So if you are in a breakout room with google meet you can reuse that link again. Here are the steps

  1. Wait for the main meeting to end
  2. Join the breakout room using the link
    You should get the breakout room has ended message.

    Right click any dimmed area that's not in the dialog, and choose "Inspect Element".
  3. Go up one element as shown in the gif below, right click, and choose delete element
    You now have the full meeting ui.

    I have not tested this with more than myself so this might not work completley.
