Welcome to Rocket11ty

Welcome to Rocket11ty! A simple yet elegant 11ty tepmlate. But, why not other 11ty templates?

Out of the box it contains:

  • Automatic Caching
  • PWAs so your blog can be installed like an app
  • Mobile Friendly design
  • Light and Dark theme. (Provided by Halfmoon.)
  • A contact form (Only on Netlify)
  • Built-in sitemap
  • RSS feed for your blog.
  • NetlifyCMS to easily publish your blog (only on Netlify)

Now, the blog system/sitemap/RSS feed was provided by the base template which can be found here. because, why recreate something that already works?
